Our Vision

At COETA E-Cell, our vision is to be a nurturing environment for aspiring entrepreneurs. We strive to equip students with the necessary tools and resources to innovate and succeed in their entrepreneurial pursuits. By fostering a culture of creativity and collaboration, we aim to inspire students to explore new ideas and solutions that address real-world challenges. We believe that by empowering students to transform their ideas into impactful startups, we can contribute meaningfully to both society and the economy. Our mission is to create a thriving hub where innovation flourishes, and students can connect, learn, and grow as future leaders in the entrepreneurial landscape.

About COETA

College of Engineering and Technology, Akola (COETA) is a renowned institution nurturing thousands of engineering minds with a commitment to excellence, innovation, and holistic development. We encourage not only academics but also innovation through various student-led activities.

What is E-Cell?

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Bombay is a non-profit student organization that encourages students to develop an entrepreneurial mindset. It supports and guides individuals who want to turn their ideas into startups. The cell organizes E-Summit, one of Asia's largest entrepreneurship festivals. It includes talks by successful entrepreneurs, startup expos, workshops, and competitions that draw participants from across the country. One of its major initiatives is Eureka!, India's largest business model competition. It provides mentorship, resources, and funding opportunities to aspiring entrepreneurs.

Our Initiatives

From ideation to execution — our workshops, pitch competitions, hackathons, startup expos, and speaker sessions are designed to support entrepreneurial journeys.
